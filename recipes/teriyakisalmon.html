Teriyaki Salmon

Description

A fresh salmon marinated in the savoury and sweet teriyaki sauce.

Adding a portion of rice and vegetables, this will make for a delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Sesame oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spices: ground ginger, mustard, and garlic powder

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients, except the salmon, in a saucepan on the stove; set one quarter aside for basting.
  2. Marinate the salmon for a maximum of two hours.
  3. Broil the marinated salmon until it flakes while basting with the leftover marinade.
