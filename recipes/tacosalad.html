Taco Salad

Description

Traditional tacos in the form of a salad! That's the take for this recipe.

People can enjoy building their own taco salads, just like they would with conventional tacos.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Beans
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Crushed tortilla chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Seasoning
  • Dressing or guacamole

Steps

  1. Cook the beef.
  2. Stir in the beans and seasoning
  3. Add crushed chips into a bowl.
  4. Top the chips with lettuce, tomatoes, and the cheese.
  5. Add the mince mixture to the bowl.
  6. Finish with dressing or guacamole.
