Chocolate Brownies

Description

Simple, delicious, chocolate brownies.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Cocoa powder
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 175C.
  2. Melt butter in a large saucepan, let cool.
  3. Whisk in the cocoa, sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Stir in the eggs, one at a time then add the flour.
  5. Bake in the oven, 20-25 minutes.
  6. Let cool for a minimum of 45 minutes.
